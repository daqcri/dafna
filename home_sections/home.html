The main goal of DAFNA project is to design a scalable and accurate truth discovery system to score the veracity of information extracted from multiple online sources.

We provide a demo of the tool we have developped, called AllegatorTrack.

Existing truth discovery models have limited applicability and we extensively studied them in our experimental evaluation study.

We release an API so that users can test the methods on their own.

Currently, we are working on real-world applications of truth discovery methods to improve open information extraction and to verify the veracity of facts related to real events from social media.
